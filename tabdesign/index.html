Home

Welcome to the Alpha v0.1 of TAB Design's Resource Centre. we've created this site to better store and share useful information across the team.

The idea is to get as much of our knowledge on here as possible so new starters can get up to speed as quickly as possible. It will also hopefully make the shared drive redundent.

It's a 000.1 release so do let me know any feedback or suggestions you have by dropping an email to Scott.

New to the design team

As a new starter to the design team, over the next few days you'll get to meet the team and settle in to your new job. We've created this section to collect together key information you will need to make the transition from new starter to old hand as easy
as possible.


But before you get stuck in, you can find below an induction essentials deck: everything you need to know to hit the ground running. If you have a question or simply want to give some feedback, contact our HR Manager, Sandrine.
